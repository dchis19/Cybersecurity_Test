25. You are a security admin for XYZ company. You suspect that company e-mails using the default POP and IMAP e-mail protocols and ports are getting intercepted while in transit. Which of the following ports should you consider using?
A) Ports 995 and 993
B) Ports 53 and 22
C) Ports 110 and 143
D) Ports 161 and 141
Answer: A
